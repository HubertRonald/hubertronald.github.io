Think, Plan, and Prototype

Take the time to think about what you are doing. Plan it out. Discuss it with others (and in the discussion prompt that follows this reading), and prototype your ideas in sandbox levels. Once you get the prototype to your liking, you can prefab out sections of the sandbox level and then use the prefabs to create your actual levels.
